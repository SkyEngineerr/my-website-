LOAN CALCULATOR

People constantly take out loans to buy things like home or car. Naturally, there is no need for pencil and paper to calculate this. Nowadays, loan calculation is one of the most sought-after topics on google.

In this mini project, I tried to make one of the loan calculation tools you see on google. I used pure Javascript for this. I used bootstrap for the design part. I also added a loading gif to make it look more sophisticated during the calculation :)
